App Instructions: Present Happiness Therapy

Welcome

"Present Happiness Therapy" is designed to guide you on a journey of self-discovery and mindfulness. Through reflecting on your past, savoring the present, and envisioning a fulfilling future, this app helps you cultivate joy and well-being in every aspect of your life.

How to Use the App

  • Past Joys & Achievements: Delve into your past to unearth moments that filled you with joy and pride. Reflect deeply on these memories to understand the sources of your happiness. Example: Revisiting the day you graduated with honors, feeling a profound sense of achievement and recognition.

  • Present Happiness: Capture and appreciate the everyday moments that bring joy to your life. Be present and mindful of the happiness that unfolds daily. Example: Enjoying a moment of laughter with friends, cherishing the sense of belonging and shared joy.

  • Mindful Futures: Set intentions for your future that resonate with your inner joy and aspirations. Plan and visualize your path to achieving personal fulfillment and well-being. Example: Dreaming of creating a community garden, fostering connectivity with nature and your local community.

  • Meditation: Use the meditation feature to center your thoughts and emotions. Focus on the rhythmic expansion and contraction of the sphere, aligning your breath to restore calmness and focus.

Tips for a Better Experience

  • Dedicate time each day to this app, making mindfulness and self-reflection a consistent practice in your life.

  • Utilize the meditation tool for mental clarity and relaxation, especially during moments of stress or when needing to refocus.

  • Regular reflections and updates in the app can serve as a powerful tool for observing your evolution and adapting your path to happiness.
